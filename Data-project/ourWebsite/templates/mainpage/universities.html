Lebanon served as an educational center for the MENA region for several years. This country has maintained this advanced educational system structure by well-training its teachers, as a result it has been known as the center for arts and culture in the Middle East. Lebanon is where a special interest in education and thriving for excellence takes place which is apparent by the number of prestigious universities that offer a variety of fields to study.

Below is a list of some universities in Lebanon.





American University of Beirut

About AUB

Founded in 1866, the American University of Beirut is a teaching-centered research university based on the American liberal arts model of higher education. AUB has over 8,000 students and 800 faculty members.

Location

AUB is located in the capital of Lebanon, Beirut. The University campus is located in the area of Ras Beirut.

Lebanese American University

About LAU

The Lebanese American University was founded in 1835 and has more than 8,500 students enrolled and is a member of the Association of American International Colleges and Universities.

Location

LAU has two campuses in Lebanon: Beirut and Byblos. In addition, LAU's New York Headquarters and academic center are located in midtown Manhattan, in close proximity to the United Nations.

Saint Joseph University

About USJ

Saint-Joseph University of Beirut is a private Lebanese university, founded in 1875 by the Society of Jesus. USJ is one of the oldest and main french university in Lebanon.

Location

USJ provides education on Beirut Campus, as well as its regional centers located in Tripoli, Zahle and Sidon.

Holy Spirit University of Kaslik

About USEK

USEK was founded in 1950 and ratified under the new Higher Education Law of 1962. USEK is the first university in Lebanon to be established as a private non-profit institution by Lebanese citizens .

Location

USEK main campus is located in Kaslik. In addition, USEK has three regional university centers located in Zahle, Chekka and Rmeich.

The University of Balamand

About UOB

The University of Balamand was founded in 1988. It offers its students hands-on application of theories, and relevant trainings in their respective fields.

Location

The University of Balamand has five campuses across Lebanon: Al koura Campus, Dekouaneh Campus, Faculty of Science Campus, Akkar Campus and Aley Campus.

Notre Dame University

About NDU

Notre Dame University-Louaize (NDU) is a private, Lebanese non-profit Catholic institution of higher education. NDU is accredited by the New England Commission of Higher Education (NECHE).
